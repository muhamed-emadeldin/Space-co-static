About Us

Get to know our company and our values

Our Story

Spaces Co. is a premier contract furnishing company specializing in hospitality, corporate, and residential environments. We create innovative furnishing solutions that balance aesthetics, functionality, and durability through our comprehensive FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures, and Equipment) services.

With extensive experience in both local and international markets, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for clients seeking high-quality, tailored furnishing solutions. Our team of experienced professionals works closely with clients, architects, and designers to ensure that every project is completed to the highest standards.

Our turnkey approach ensures a seamless experience from concept development through installation and aftercare, making us the partner of choice for discerning clients across the region.

Our Mission

To create exceptional furnishing solutions that transform spaces and exceed client expectations through innovative design, quality craftsmanship, and dedicated service.

Our Vision

To be the leading contract furnishing company in the region, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction in every project we undertake.
