Our Story

At SPACES CONTRACT, we transform hospitality, office, and residential environments with top-tier FF&E solutions. As representatives of leading furniture manufacturers in Turkey, China, and Europe, we deliver exceptional quality and craftsmanship.

We know each project is unique, and we collaborate closely with clients to create tailored solutions that align with their style and needs. Partner with us to craft inspiring spaces that enhance experiences and elevate lifestyles.

With a strong technical, production, and installation team, we have extensive experience in large-scale projects across Saudi Arabia and the Middle East, ensuring attention to detail from design through to installation.

Our Mission

To create exceptional furnishing solutions that transform spaces and exceed client expectations through innovative design, quality craftsmanship, and dedicated service.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative, high-quality furnishing solutions, transforming spaces across industries into environments that inspire, engage, and elevate experiences for every client.
