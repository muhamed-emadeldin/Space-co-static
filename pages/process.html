Our Process

How we work, what we've accomplished, and what we believe in

What Sets Us Apart

At SPACES CONTRACT we believe that a successful furnishing project is built on a foundation of collaboration and precision. We guide our clients through a comprehensive process that ensures every detail is tailored to their needs. Here’s how we work together from start to finish:

At Spaces Co., we follow a structured approach to ensure that every project is completed efficiently, on time, and to the highest standards. Our proven process has been refined over years of experience in the contract furnishing industry.

Design Service

We begin by understanding your vision and requirements. Our design team collabo- rates with you to create innovative concepts that align with the brand and aesthetic preferences.

Measurement

Once the design is finalized, we conduct precise measurements of the space. This critical step ensures that every piece of furniture will fit perfectly within the intended environment.

Customized offer

We tailor your price offer to match your budget while we keep an eye on the look and feel of the design .

Made-to-Measure Mockup

To bring your vision to life, we create custom mockups if required of selected elements. This allows you to visualize the design in your space , confirm the materials and make any necessary adjustments before proceeding.

Project management

Our project management approach ensures a seamless experience from start to finish. We start with a detailed consultation to understand your vision, then develop a project plan and timeline to ensure on-time delivery and smooth coordination at every stage.

Mass Production

Upon approval of the mockups, we move into mass production. Our factories utilizes high-quality materials and craftsmanship to ensure that each piece meets rigorous standards.

Quality Control

Throughout the production process, we conduct thorough quality control checks. This guarantees that every item is not only beautifully crafted but also durable and functional.

Shipping and Logistics

Once production is complete, we handle all shipping and logistics. Our team ensures that your furnishings arrive on time and in perfect condi- tion.

Installation

Our experienced installation team then steps in to set up your space. We take great care in arranging everything to maximize both aesthetics and functionality.

After Handover Support

Our commitment to you doesn’t end at installation. We provide ongoing support after handover, including maintenance and addressing any needs that may arise to ensure your complete Client satisfaction.

Turnkey Supply and Installation
At SPACES CONTRACT, we are your comprehensive solution for furnish- ing and decor needs, offering tailored turnkey services to meet your unique requirements. Years of collaboration with hospitality leaders and international designers have enriched our industry expertise, allowing us to continuously elevate our offerings.
By working closely with you, we ensure every detail aligns with your vision, creating spaces that authentically reflect your style. Our com- mitment to innovation and excellence means you can trust us to deliver results that exceed expectations. our experience help select the correct alternatives wherever is needed.

Tailored Solutions

We understand that every project is unique. Our customizable options allow you to select materials, designs, and finishes .

Expert Craftsmanship

Our team of skilled artisans brings years of experience and a passion for quality to every piece of furniture we create.

Comprehensive Service

We provide a full suite of services, from initial design consultation to final installation. Our dedicated team is with you every step of the way, ensuring a smooth and seamless experience.

Quality Assurance

Our rigorous quality control processes guarantee that every product meets the highest standards of durability and functionality. We stand behind our work, ensuring your complete satisfaction.

Competitive Pricing

You can trust that you’re receiving the best value for your investment. We work hard to provide cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality.

Guarantee

We guarantee client satisfaction through our continues support during the project journey and after project handover .
