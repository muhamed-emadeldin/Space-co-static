What Sets Us Apart

At Spaces Co., our unique approach combines innovative design, expert craftsmanship, and a commitment to excellence. Learn more about how we work, our experience, and the values that guide everything we do.

At Spaces Co., we follow a structured approach to ensure that every project is completed efficiently, on time, and to the highest standards. Our proven process has been refined over years of experience in the contract furnishing industry.

Design

We begin by understanding your vision, requirements, and budget. Our design team collaborates with you to develop furniture and furnishing solutions that align with your aesthetic preferences and functional needs.

Measurement

Our technical team conducts precise measurements of your space to ensure perfect fit and integration of all furniture and furnishing elements. This critical step prevents issues during installation.

Production

Based on approved designs and measurements, we begin production in our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities. Our skilled craftsmen utilize premium materials and techniques to create high-quality furnishings.

Installation

Our experienced installation team ensures that every element is precisely installed according to the design specifications. We coordinate all logistics and minimize disruption to your operations.

Aftercare

Our commitment extends beyond installation. We provide comprehensive aftercare services to maintain the quality and longevity of your furnishings, ensuring your continued satisfaction.

With our extensive experience in contract furnishing for hospitality, corporate, and residential environments, we have developed the expertise to deliver tailored turnkey solutions that meet the highest quality standards.
