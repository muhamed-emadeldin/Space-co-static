Our Facilities

Our comprehensive production facility includes specialized departments to handle every aspect of fabrication.

Woodworking Department

Our woodworking department is equipped with precision machinery for cutting, shaping, and finishing wood components. From custom millwork to intricate joinery, our skilled craftsmen create exceptional wooden elements for any project.

Metal Fabrication

Our metal fabrication area features cutting-edge welding equipment, CNC metal cutting machines, and forming tools. This allows us to create custom metal components with precision and durability for various applications.

Finishing Department

Our dedicated finishing department ensures that every piece receives the perfect finish. From paint and stain applications to specialized treatments, we achieve consistent, high-quality finishes that protect and enhance our products.

Assembly Area

In our spacious assembly area, components come together to create finished products. Our meticulous assembly process ensures that every piece meets our exacting standards before leaving the factory for installation.
